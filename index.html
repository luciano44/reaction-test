The Speed of Human Reaction Time

Human reaction time, how fast we respond to things, is impressive. It usually takes about 200 to 300 milliseconds for our brains to process and react to what we see. Athletes and trained individuals often react faster because of practice and anticipation. Our senses, brains, and bodies work together to make this happen. Understanding reaction time is important in fields like sports and safety.
